Discover our history

EPC techspace, formerly known only as "Excellent Programmers Community" was founded in 2020 on the 25th of July by Steve, Dani, Culani, Mdluli and Dalue. After a failed name search, the word "techspace" was added to the name "EPC" to form "Excellent programmers community techspace". The company was then established on the 21st of October 2021 under the name "epcTechspace"
